DET-001_dns_tunneling.spl
index=nextdns
| eval domain_length=len(domain)
| eval subdomain=mvindex(split(domain,"."),0)
| eval subdomain_length=len(subdomain)
| where domain_length > 50 OR subdomain_length > 30
| bin _time span=5m
| stats count as queries, dc(domain) as unique_domains,
        avg(domain_length) as avg_length by _time, client_ip
| where queries > 20 OR unique_domains > 15
| eval severity=case(queries>50,"CRITICAL",queries>20,"HIGH",1=1,"MEDIUM")
| table _time, client_ip, queries, unique_domains, avg_length, severity
| sort - severity
[*] NOTE
Evaluates domain and subdomain length per 5-minute bucket. Flags clients exceeding query volume or unique domain thresholds. Severity tiered: CRITICAL (>50 queries), HIGH (>20), MEDIUM baseline.
