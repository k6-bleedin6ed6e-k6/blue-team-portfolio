[ hypothesis ]
High-entropy or unusually long domain names in home network DNS logs may indicate malware C2 communication or DNS tunneling activity. Legitimate traffic rarely requires subdomains exceeding 30 characters.
[ hunt query ]
SPL · index=nextdns
index=nextdns earliest=-7d
| eval domain_length=len(domain)
| stats count by domain, domain_length
| where domain_length > 40
| sort - domain_length
| head 20
